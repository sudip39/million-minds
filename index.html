About us

The Accelerate - startup talks conducted across college campuses are recorded and put into an organised form so as to make it accessible to interested students. This huge repository of startup knowledge is named AXIOM. To gain full access to the AXIOM program and equip students with optimal knowledge resources, colleges need to register with us. Once the college gets access to the AXIOM package, students can create their own accounts and access all the videos. On successful completion of the AXIOM Entrepreneurship Program, the student will be entitled to a certificate from Millionminds.
